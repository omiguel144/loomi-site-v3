How we curate

The 90% rule.
Every time.

Every listing on Loomi meets a single threshold: at least 90% natural fibers — cotton, linen, merino wool, cashmere, silk, or hemp. We check every product against this standard before it appears, and show the fiber breakdown clearly on every page.

✦︎ Loomi Pick

We award this badge to brands that hold independent third-party certification — GOTS, B-Corp, Bluesign, or Fair Trade — on top of our 90% threshold. Not every brand on Loomi is certified; the badge tells you which ones are.
